What RevenueSure Does

The platform provides several functionalities, with the main ones being
Billing

Enables generation of invoices for customers based on specific needs, and is integrated with the chart of accounts to enable accurate referencing of charge codes and for reconciliation

Collection

Provides channels of collection for both structured and unstructured revenue streams through mobile (App and USSD), web, devices (terminals), and agency networks (banks).

Receipting

provides receipts for all payments received as proof of payment. Each type of payment will have its own customized receipts. Each department has it’s own portal for service delivery.

Customer Service & Issuance

Provides service portals where customers can obtain certificates, licenses and permits.

Reporting

The platform provides detailed dashboards the report on revenue collection and management activities. Collections are displayed on a real-time dashboard, and data is presented in analytical format to show the status of revenue management for the county.

Billing and Collections Agency

To enhance reach of services, the platform provides agency services for billing and collection of payments. This is implemented either as a standalone system, or as part of an existing agency network such as a bank.


The agency services provider is allowed to recruit agents e.g. a bank can enable new or existing agents to provide services. Agents will get customized devices that enable provision of services (billing, receipting and issuance).

  • Landing Page

    Landing page, shows the agent the float they have, and they can navigate to billing, query bill from biller or top-up their float

  • Merchants

    Agent selects the biller for whom to generate new bill, or query existing bill

  • Billing

    For new bill, agent enters all required information via drop-down menus and data entry where necessary

  • Bill Payment

    Once bill is generated, agent selects payment mode and confirms

  • Digital Receipt

    A digital receipt is issued for the bill payment

  • The Physical Receipt

    Have a look at a physical receipt.

Revenue Enforcement

Our platform provides enforcement, monitoring and supervision applications that provide status of revenue assets e.g. parking, businesses. To enable effective enforcement and supervision, the platform provides the following functions:


Enforcement App

allows querying of specific data to confirm status e.g. car number plate for parking and business ID for businesses. Can be a mobile app or USSD.

Service Portals

Allows officers to analyse information of various revenue generating assets and assign the per location e.g. sub-county, ward, service points etc.

Compliance Reports

Provides reports on expected payments that have gone past due that need follow-up.

Revenue Management

Our platform will enable the entire organization to manage all revenue activities. This will be done by:


  • Providing a single point of view for all services offered and payments received by the organization. All payments will be automatically reconciled with organization records where available.

  • Providing analytics with insights on trends, patterns and provision of organization services. This includes statistics on users and their requests.
