Key Services

The platform provides several functionalities, with the main ones being
Billing

This is an advanced billing platform that generates invoices for customers whenever they need to make a payment to the revenue authority. This enables the authority to track and report on all collections.

Collection

The platform hosts channels of collection for both structured and unstructured revenue streams. Collection channels include mobile (App and USSD), web, devices (terminals), and agency networks (banks).

Receipting

The platform provides receipts for all payments received as proof of payment. Each type of payment will have its own customized receipts.

Customer Service & Issuance

Each revenue stream will be supported with a service portal where customers can obtain certificates, licenses and permits.

Reporting

The platform provides detailed dashboards the report on revenue collection and management activities. Collections are displayed on a real-time dashboard, and data is presented in analytical format to show the status of revenue management for the county.

Revenue Collection

Our platform supports an authority to collect revenue using the following methods

Web portal

Citizens are able to create unique profiles on the authority’s official web portal. This portal provides a breakdown of all digitized services that Citizens can pay online
See more

Mobile app

Citizens will download the government services payment app which will contain all digitized services e.g. business permits, parking, land rates and rents.
See more

Hand-held devices

Our handheld devices will support collection of revenues e.g. parking, bus parks, markets, ferry etc. They will also be able to issue receipts, licenses and permits on the spot.
See more

Banks and agents

We will provide our platform to banks and agents for Citizens to pay government fees through their channels e.g. branches, mobile banking, internet banking etc.

Revenue Enforcement

To ensure that all citizens pay for government services, we will implement several applications and methods to monitor, verify and ensure adherence to payments for services in the entire authority. These are:

Handheld devices

Our devices will enable enforcement officers to scan vehicle number plates, stickers and receipts to confirm parking fees were paid.

Mapping of all revenue assets

We will tag all assets of the County using GIS and provide a map with details of who has paid and who has not paid

Real-time dashboards and analysis

We will provide dashboards that will enable specific revenue streams to follow up on due and overdue accounts

Revenue Management

To ensure that all citizens pay for government services, we will implement several applications and methods to monitor, verify and ensure adherence to payments for services in the entire authority. These are:

  • Providing a single point of view for all services offered and payments received by the County. All payments will be automatically reconciled with government records where available.

Our value proposition

Opex models

We implement our platform using an opex model, where the initial expenditure by the revenue authority is minimal, as we recover our costs of implementation through transactional fees. Our models are agile to suit the Authority’s requirements.

Revenue mapping

As part of the implementation, we will conduct a detailed assessment of authority’s administrative area and map all revenue assets. We will present this information on a GIS map showing collection points, collection and enforcement officers, as well as devices.

Reporting

We will provide real-time dashboards that update all payments as soon as they are paid. This dashboard will be able to generate analytical information showing daily, monthly, quarterly and annual trends in parking revenue collection.

Knowledge transfer

We will train the relevant Authority’s staff to ensure that they can fully operate and manage the platform to provide necessary support. We will also ensure the staff are able to respond rapidly to any problems or requests from the Citizens or leadership.
